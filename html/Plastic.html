Plastic

Why is reducing the use of plastic important?

By the year 2050 plastic is expected to overpopulate the amount of fish in the ocean and in addition to that 99 percent of all seabirds on the planet will have consume some. With eight million tonnes ( a metric ton thats is equal to 2,205 pounds) of plastic going into the ocean each year
