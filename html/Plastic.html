Plastic

Why is reducing the use of plastic important?

By the year 2050 plastic is expected to overpopulate the amount of fish in the ocean, and in addition to that, 99 percent of all seabirds on the planet will have consume some. Over 600 species are harmed by plastic for example, sea turtles see plastic bags as jellyfish and think they are okay to eat. And to baby perch the plastic bags appear as a tastier snack than plankon, their normal diet. With 8.3 tonnes ( a metric ton thats is equal to 2,205 pounds) of plastic going into the ocean each year some say we could be entering the sixth mass extinxtion of life on Earth.
